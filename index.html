How to play

Use the arrows (L) (arrow image) and (R) (arrow image) to move Goofy

Commands: arrows: Left and Right. Space bar for jump

To win the game just collect as many dogs possible

You get points by collecting dogs and lose them by collecting bananas

Score: 0

Goal

Help Goofy rescue the dogs.

Avoid collecting bananas as Goofy will slip and fall.
